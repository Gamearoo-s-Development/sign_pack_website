How to update (portable ZIP)

  • 1) Close Signpack Maker.
  • 2) Click Get Latest Portable ZIP (above).
  • 3) Extract the ZIP.
  • 4) Move/replace the extracted files into your existing portable folder (the same folder as your current Signpack Maker.exe). Allow overwrites.
  • 5) Reopen the app. Done!
📦
Where are my sign packs?
Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\SignpackMaker\packs
Updating the app won’t touch this folder.

Quick install steps

1
Click Download Latest (.zip) above.
2
Extract the ZIP to a folder of your choice.
3
Run Signpack Maker.exe from the extracted folder.
4
Your packs are stored at %LOCALAPPDATA%\SignpackMaker\packs.
